Preserving the Legacy of Kasaragod Sarees

Lakshmi Devi

Lakshmi Devi is a master weaver renowned for her expertise in crafting traditional Kasaragod sarees using time-honored handloom techniques. The art of weaving in Kasaragod dates back to the 18th century, with the Shaliya community playing a pivotal role in its development. Kasaragod sarees are distinguished by their vibrant colors, intricate borders crafted using Jacquard or Dobby techniques, and the use of high thread counts and vat dyes, ensuring durability and brilliance. Lakshmi Devi's commitment to preserving these traditional methods not only maintains the cultural heritage of Kasaragod but also supports the local economy by training and employing community members. Her dedication ensures that the legacy of Kasaragod's weaving tradition continues to thrive, offering authentic and high-quality sarees that reflect the region's rich cultural tapestry.
